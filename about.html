About Zombit Farmer

A pixel-art farm gone wrong — and a community of players who kept it running anyway.

🧟 The Story

Zombit Farmer started as a quiet little homestead. Then the mine underneath it cracked open, and something started climbing out at night. Now every farmer plants crops by day, boards up the barn by dusk, and heads into the mine to fight for resources when the sun goes down. It's a farming game with teeth — cozy on the surface, dangerous underneath.

🎮 What You'll Find In-Game

🌾

Farming

Plant, water, and harvest crops through multiple growth stages on your own plot of land.

⛏️

The Mine

Descend into procedurally dangerous tunnels to gather ore and fight off what's living down there.

🐔

Pets & Mounts

Raise animal companions and unlock mounts that speed up your farm work.

🏗️

Building

Expand your homestead — furniture, storage, and defenses against nightly raids.

🛒

Shop & Market

Buy, sell, and trade goods, plus a gem shop and NFT shop for cosmetics and boosts.

🏆

Battle Pass & Quests

Work through seasonal quests and a battle pass track for exclusive rewards.

🔗 Built on Solana

Zombit Farmer connects directly to your Solana wallet — no separate account, no email sign-up. Connect once, and your identity in the game is your wallet address. We support Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, and other Solana Wallet Standard wallets, so you can play with whichever wallet you already trust.

👥 The Community

Zombit Farmer is built and shaped by the people who play it. Bug reports, feature ideas, and screenshots from the field all feed back into the game. Share your farm, tag your best (and worst) mine runs, and help decide what gets built next.

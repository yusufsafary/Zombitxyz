Quick Start Guide

How to Play

Everything you need to get your farm running and survive your first night.

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Connect your wallet. Click the wallet button in the top corner of the game and choose Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, or another Solana wallet. Approve the connection request in your wallet's popup.
  2. Pick your nickname. Your wallet address becomes your identity, but you can set a display name so other farmers can recognize you.
  3. Walk onto your plot. Use your movement controls to step onto an empty tile on your farm.
  4. Plant your first crop. Open your inventory, select a seed, and plant it on a tilled tile. Crops grow through visible stages — check back as they mature.
  5. Harvest and sell. Once a crop is fully grown, harvest it and sell it at the market for gold, or hang on to ingredients you'll need later.

🎯 Core Loop

☀️

Day — Farm

Plant, water, and harvest crops. Manage pets and mounts. Build and upgrade structures.

🌙

Night — Survive

Reinforce your farm before dusk. Watch for raids and be ready to defend your homestead.

⛏️

Anytime — Mine

Head underground for ore and rare drops. Deeper levels pay off more but carry more risk.

🕹️ Controls & UI

  • Inventory — manage seeds, tools, and collected resources.
  • Shop / Market — buy seeds and supplies, or sell your harvest.
  • Gem Shop / NFT Shop — spend gems on cosmetics and boosts.
  • Bank — store gold and goods safely.
  • Skills — spend earned points to improve your farmer's abilities.
  • Quests & Battle Pass — track objectives and season rewards.
  • Chicken Run — jump into a quick mini-game for extra rewards.
  • Chat — talk to other farmers online.

🔐 Wallet Tips

  • Keep a small amount of SOL in your wallet — some actions need a tiny network fee.
  • You can disconnect and reconnect at any time from the wallet menu; your progress is tied to your wallet address, not your device.
  • Only approve transactions you initiated yourself from inside the game.

🆘 Stuck?

If your wallet won't connect, make sure the extension is installed and unlocked, then refresh the page and try again. For anything else, reach out through the game's chat or community channels — the tags @ZombitFarmersGG and #SolanaGaming are the fastest way to get noticed.
